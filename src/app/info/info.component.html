Typeshift is an excelent anagram/crossword/wordsearch style puzzle game for iOS created by Zach Gage. You can and should download the game here.

Cheat at Typeshift is a project by Sam Gardner, you can fork the Angular app here or be LinkedIn frineds with Sam here.

Cheat at Typeshift checks for solution words against the npm package word-list-json by Calvin Metcalf.

To produce the possible solutions for a puzzle, recreate the columns and letters of the puzzle

You can either add the letters you have left in a puzzle you've been working on, or recreate a full puzzle.

Click or tap the triangle on the left of the starting 'a' to add a column.

Click or tap the triangle below a column to add another letter to that column.

Once your letters are in, click the 'Get Solutions' button.

You can click/tap the x icon by a solution word to check off the letters used and reorder the solutions for efficiency
